1. Information Collection and Use

FrameCraft Pro is designed to be a private, ad-free experience. We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

2. Ad-Free Experience

FrameCraft Pro is a "No-Ads" application. We do not use third-party advertising networks, and therefore, no data is shared with advertisers for tracking or profiling purposes.

3. Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since all image processing happens on your device, your photos remain under your control. We remind you to only share your created frames with trusted individuals on social media.
