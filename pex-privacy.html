Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** September 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the **Personal Expenses App** (the "App") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the mobile application.

1. The Offline Nature of the App - Your Financial Privacy

The core function of the **Personal Expenses App** is to be an entirely **offline-first** personal finance tracker. This means:

  • Financial Data Stays on Your Device: All of your personal expense records, weekly/monthly/yearly insights, and transaction data are stored locally on your mobile device.
  • No Server Uploads: We **do not** transmit, store, or have access to your financial transactions, categories, or personal expense amounts on any remote server.
  • No Internet Required for Core Functionality: You can use the app's primary features (tracking, managing, and viewing insights) without an internet connection.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We are a privacy-focused application. We **do not** collect, store, or transmit the following information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • Your financial data, transaction details, or account balances.
  • Any sensitive information from your device, such as contacts, photos, or location.

This data is technical (e.g., type of device, version of OS, crash report) and is used **only** to fix bugs and improve stability. It **does not** include your financial data or any personal identifiers.
  • Usage Analytics (Anonymized): We may use tools (such as [e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase]) to understand general usage trends (e.g., which screen is most popular, how many users open the app). This data is completely anonymized and aggregated and **cannot** be used to identify you or your specific financial records.
